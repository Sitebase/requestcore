Using the documentation
  • Press up, down, left and right to navigate the browse panel. Press enter to make a selection. Press esc to clear searches.
  • Searching will intelligently find results that start with (first), contain (second) and smart match (third) the words you're looking for.
  • Looking for a method that exists in multiple classes? Narrow your results by following your search term with the class you want to search.
  • Tested with Safari 4, Firefox 3, Opera 9.5, Chrome, IE8. IE 6 & 7 are usable but painfully slow.
